About

Dnyanada Ramtirthkar (born 26 June 1996) is an Indian television and film actress. She made her television debut with Sakhya Re (2017), and her debut in film with Dhurala (2020). In 2021, she portrayed a role of "Apoorva" in Star Pravah's Thipkyanchi Rangoli.Ramtirthkar was born in Vita, Sangli. However, she currently lives in Pune and Mumbai. She completed her secondary education in Pune from P.E.S. Modern Girls High School.
